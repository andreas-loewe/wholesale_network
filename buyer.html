How it works

Wholesalers, who have limited budgets, advertise for home sellers and negotiate contracts below market value. Once under contract, they look to assign or resell their deals to people like you who have enough cash to make the purchase or the repairs, which are outside their budget. They collect an assignment fee when they sell the deal do you.

Why this site exists

My wife, Tarah, and I attended a FortuneBuilders seminar, and we immediately became enveloped in the wonder of real estate investing. We got to work looking for people to sell us deals. In the process of building our list of wholesalers, we realized that it would be a lot easier if wholesalers came to us instead.

When we advertised for Wholesalers we met lots of investors who had deals outside our area. It was such a shame to say goodbye to those good deals and see them go to waste.

We had the idea that by creating a central list of people like us, we could attract a lot of wholesalers with a lot of deals, and even though those deals would not all be right for us, we would be copied on those deals that were right, and that would be a valuable thing to help our business grow!

Since we started this site, the list has grown tremendously. Because of people like you, this site has become a must-stop location for wholesalers who are looking to close quickly. By using this site to send out a deal notice, they are instantly connected to thousands of people like us who have a chance to review the deal and make an offer. As a result, now that we are on the list, Tarah and I no longer hunt for wholesale dealers. Now, we sit back and check our e-mail and let the deals come to us!
