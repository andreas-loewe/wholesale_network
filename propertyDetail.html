Tips for Selling your deal fast

Deals that are picked up quickly have one or more of the following qualities:

  • Home price plus estimated repair cost is 70% of ARV (After repair value), so buyers hear and see room for profit.
  • Video links to YouTube show a walk-through of the home outside and inside, so buyers feel like they have seen the property.
  • Cash is sent to a verifiable escrow company and NOT directly to seller, so buyers trust that they will not loose their cash investment.
  • Be thorough so that buyers have full information.
Tip: Have a deal ready to sell before you continue.
